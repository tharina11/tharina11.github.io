About me

Hello! I’m a data analyst who believes that numbers only matter when they connect to real-world impact. I'm driven by a desire to bring clarity, empathy, and ethical insight to complex problems — and to work with organizations whose mission aligns with those values.

I explore data analytics, modeling, and engineering across diverse domains, including geospatial, financial, and agricultural data. I enjoy problem-solving, learning new technologies and domains, and tackling code challenges with curiosity and persistence.
